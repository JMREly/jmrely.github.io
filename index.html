How it works

Focus. Bloom. Repeat.

Every focus session advances your cherry blossom garden through 12 stages. Your progress is permanent — come back tomorrow and pick up exactly where you left off.

Step 01
Set your session

Choose a countdown duration — 25, 50 minutes, or custom — or switch to stopwatch mode for open-ended focus with no time pressure.

Step 02
Watch your garden grow

As you focus, your garden advances through 12 stages — from bare branches to full cherry blossom bloom. Each session adds to the same persistent world.

Step 03
Build something lasting

Your garden never resets. It's a permanent record of your focus — the more you work, the more beautiful it becomes.
